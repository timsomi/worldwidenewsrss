Crashed?

If Crash Reporter appears when World Wide News RSS crashes, please provide as many details as possible to help developers.

  • In World Wide News RSS, go to Help > More Troubleshooting Information, scroll to "Crash Reports", click a recent report link to open the crash report page.
  • Click the Bugzilla tab, then click on an existing bug report under "Related Bugs" and add a comment to the bug, or click "World Wide News RSS" to submit a new bug report.
